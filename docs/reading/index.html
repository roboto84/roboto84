I mostly read articles to keep up with current events, but once in a while I'll be lucky enough to discover an interesting book that I can get into a flow with. Most of the time the books I like are sci-fi based, but any book with a plethora of interesting technical ideas and novel concepts are fair game. Such books serve as a fuel source and harbringer of my future interests/projects.
